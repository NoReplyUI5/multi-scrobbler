Vendetta Plugin

RPC

Show off your music status from Last.fm or ListenBrainz on your Discord profile with comprehensive console logging.

View on GitHub Install Plugin
🎧

Multiple Services

Supports Last.fm and ListenBrainz. Switch between services anytime from settings.

🖼️

Album Art

Automatically fetches album covers from Cover Art Archive and Last.fm for your Discord RPC.

⏱️

Live Timestamps

Shows track progress and duration in your Discord activity when available.

⚙️

Fully Customizable

Customize app name, tooltip text, update interval, ignore list, and more.

How to Install

  1. Open Discord with Vendetta or Bunny installed
  2. Go to Settings > RPC
  3. Select your service (Last.fm or ListenBrainz)
  4. Enter your credentials and enable
